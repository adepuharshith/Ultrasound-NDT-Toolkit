📚 Resources

Textbooks, landmark papers, software, standards, and online tools for ultrasonic NDE

📚

Ultrasonic Testing of Materials — Krautkrämer & Krautkrämer

The definitive reference for conventional UT. Covers wave physics, transducers, testing techniques, and defect evaluation. Often called "the bible of UT."

Textbook Foundation
📚

Ultrasonic Waves in Solid Media — Joseph L. Rose

Comprehensive treatment of guided wave theory and applications. Essential for Lamb waves, pipe inspection, and phased-array analysis. Rose is at Penn State.

Textbook Guided Waves
📚

Fundamentals of Ultrasonic Phased Arrays — Lester Schmerr

Systematic treatment of phased-array beam modeling, focal laws, and imaging. Includes MATLAB code. Schmerr is at Iowa State (CNDE).

Textbook Phased Arrays
📚

Wave Propagation in Elastic Solids — J. D. Achenbach

Rigorous mathematical treatment of elastodynamics. Required reading for anyone working with wave scattering, diffraction, and anisotropic media.

Textbook Theory
📚

Phased Array Testing — Basic Theory for Industrial Applications (Olympus NDT)

Free PDF from Olympus. Very practical — covers probe selection, wedge angles, S-scan setup, and weld inspection. Good starting point before the Schmerr book.

Textbook Free PDF Practical
📚

Nondestructive Testing Handbook, Vol. 7: Ultrasonic Testing — ASNT

The ASNT handbook series. Vol. 7 covers the full spectrum of UT methods, from pulse-echo basics to TOFD and advanced imaging. Standard reference for Level III certification.

Textbook ASNT
📄

Post-processing of the Full Matrix of Ultrasonic Transmit–Receive Array Data for Non-destructive Evaluation — Holmes, Drinkwater, Wilcox (2005)

NDT&E International. The landmark paper introducing FMC/TFM. Essential reading for anyone working with array imaging.

Paper FMC/TFM Landmark
📄

Acoustic Nonlinearity Parameter β Measurement — Review of Methods

Reviews techniques for measuring β using harmonic generation and resonance methods. Key reference for nonlinear UT / early damage detection.

Paper Nonlinear UT
📄

Ultrasonic Arrays for Non-destructive Evaluation: A Review — Drinkwater & Wilcox (2006)

NDT&E International. Broad survey of array imaging methods, beam forming, signal processing, and applications. Excellent overview paper.

Paper Review Arrays
📄

Frequency-Dependent Ultrasonic Grain Noise in Metals — Margetan, Thompson, Yalda-Mooshabad

Foundational paper on coherent grain noise statistics, frequency scaling laws, and SNR prediction. Invaluable for inspection planning in coarse-grained materials.

Paper Grain Noise SNR
📄

A Rapid Signal Processing Technique to Remove the Effect of Dispersion from Guided Wave Signals — Wilcox (2003)

IEEE T-UFFC. Introduces the Warped Frequency Transform for dispersion compensation in guided wave inspection. Widely cited in the GW community.

Paper Guided Waves Dispersion
💻

CIVA — CEA (France)

Industry-standard simulation platform for UT, ET, and RT. Models beam propagation, defect response, and phased-array imaging. Free academic licenses available.

Software Simulation Academic License
💻

COMSOL Multiphysics — Acoustics & Structural Mechanics Modules

FEM/FEA for modeling wave propagation, transducer resonance, and piezoelectric coupling. Very flexible; steep learning curve. Purdue has a campus license.

Software FEM Purdue License
💻

ARIM / PyUS — Python UT Signal Processing Libraries

Open-source Python libraries for UT data processing: A-scan filtering, TFM reconstruction, dispersion curve calculation (scikit-signal, arim). Community-maintained.

Software Open Source Python
💻

DISPERSE — Imperial College London

Software for computing dispersion curves of guided waves in layered media (plates, pipes, coatings). The standard tool for guided wave mode selection. Free for research.

Software Guided Waves Free Research
💻

MATLAB Phased Array System Toolbox

MathWorks toolbox with functions for array design, beam forming, and signal simulation. Useful for prototyping focal laws and evaluating grating lobes.

Software MATLAB Arrays
📜

ASTM E2491 — Standard Guide for Evaluating PA UT Equipment

Covers verification of phased-array systems: beam steering, focal depth, sensitivity calibration. Reference for qualifying PA UT setups.

Standard ASTM Phased Arrays
📜

ASTM E2580 — Standard Practice for TOFD

Establishes procedures for time-of-flight diffraction UT of welds. Covers probe placement, calibration, and defect sizing.

Standard ASTM TOFD
📜

ASME BPVC Section V — Article 4: Ultrasonic Examination

Governing code for UT of pressure vessels and piping. Required knowledge for anyone inspecting ASME-coded components.

Standard ASME Pressure Vessels
📜

ISO 16827 — Characterization and Sizing of Discontinuities

International standard for defect sizing by UT: 6 dB drop, 20 dB drop, DGS, and TOFD methods. Widely used in European aerospace and power generation.

Standard ISO Sizing
🌐

ASNT NDT Resource Center — e-learning

ASNT's online learning portal with UT Level I & II study materials, quizzes, and reference data. Good supplement to formal training.

Online Free Training
🌐

NDT Resource Center — Iowa State / CNDE

Comprehensive online reference by the Center for NDE at Iowa State. Covers all major NDE methods with diagrams, equations, and interactive applets.

Online Free Reference
🌐

IEEE T-UFFC — IEEE Transactions on Ultrasonics, Ferroelectrics, and Frequency Control

The primary journal for UT and acoustics research. Open access articles available. Subscribe for new publication alerts relevant to your research area.

Online Journal
🌐

NDT.net — Open Access NDT Journal & Community

Large archive of conference proceedings, journal articles, and technical reports on NDE. Many papers are freely downloadable. Good for industry-practice literature.

Online Free Archive
