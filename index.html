About this toolkit

This site is an open educational resource compiled from PhD research in ultrasonic NDE at Purdue University. It is designed for graduate students, early-career researchers, and engineers entering the field who need both conceptual grounding and practical tools — in one place.

Content is continuously updated. Contributions and corrections welcome.
